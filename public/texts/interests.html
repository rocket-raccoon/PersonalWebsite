Interests

Topics and things I enjoy learning about and screwing around in within the computational world:

  • functional programming (Scala, baby)
  • deep learning (mainly via the Theano framework although I am very interested by Google’s TensorFlow)
  • backend web development (I built this site using NodeJS + Express like all the other cool kids)
  • machine learning more generally (from time to time I’ll throw some Python at a Kaggle competition)

Outside of work, one of the biggest activities I enjoy is this nebulous thing called “fitness”. I go to the gym 3 times a week in the mornings. Squats, bench, and deadlifts. It’s all about the pump, as Arnold would say. Really though, I am usually up for any physical activity—be it bike riding, rock climbing, yoga, swimming, or whatever. I also try running when I can so my cardio is not absolutely terrible. I started lifting to look good, but, at this point, I just do it because it’s fun and a good way to clear my mind from a job existing within almost purely within the realms of the entirely logical and predictable.

I also enjoy doing some creative writing in my free time when I am alone and have not much else to do. Usually this involves settling upon some random, but interesting or provocative prompt and going with it, wherever that may lead. Writing a novel is definitely something I want to do someday even if no one reads it. We’ll see.

I am also fulfilling my long delayed goal of picking up a musical instrument. I continuously played the guitar for like 3 months straight before being swamped by grad school. I am picking that up again and doing guitar classes in the city.

And, of course, it’s New York City. So, like 95% of the people here, I find myself often drinking at some awful / great bar with my fellow aged 20-something friends on weekends or staying in watching Netflix because my face hurts from the cold.
